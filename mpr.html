Middle Pillar Ritual

Description

The Middle Pillar Ritual (MPR) is a powerful Qabalistic energy meditation used to balance, strengthen, and circulate divine energy through the body. It is often performed after the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP) to establish spiritual alignment and personal empowerment. This ritual activates the energy centers along the central column of the Tree of Life within your body.
