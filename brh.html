Banishing Ritual of the Hexagram

Description

The Banishing Ritual of the Hexagram (BRH) is a powerful ceremonial ritual designed to banish planetary influences and align the magician with higher spiritual forces. It typically follows the Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram (LBRP) and is commonly used before advanced magical workings. This ritual works with the macrocosmic forces (planetary and zodiacal), while the LBRP works with microcosmic forces (elemental).

Purpose & Benefits

The Banishing Ritual of the Hexagram serves several important functions:

Removes planetary influences and external energies
Strengthens aura and spiritual authority
Elevates consciousness by aligning with the divine
Enhances magical effectiveness when done after LBRP
Balances macrocosmic forces within your ritual space
Prepares the magician for advanced planetary workings

This ritual is recommended for:

  • Before any planetary or zodiacal magical work
  • After the LBRP to create a complete banishing sequence
  • When working with advanced invocations
  • To clear lingering planetary influences from previous workings

Materials Needed

Gather these items before beginning

Ritual wand or index finger of dominant hand
Optional: White or gold candle
Optional: Frankincense or sandalwood incense
Ritual robes or comfortable clothing
Quiet space with enough room to move in a circle
